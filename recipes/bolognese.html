Grandma's Bolognese Recipe

Home

Description
Grandma's spaghetti bolognese is a culinary embrace, weaving savory notes of slow-cooked tomatoes and rich ground beef into a tapestry of comfort.
Each tender noodle cradles the umami symphony, a family secret passed through generations.
Simmered to perfection, this dish whispers nostalgia, inviting you to a warm, cozy night at the heart of familial connection.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tin of mackerel, finely chopped
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 1 large carrots, trimmed and finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 4-6 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 2-3 sprigs rosemary leaves picked and finely chopped
  • 500g pork mince

    • Grandma's Secret Sauce
  • 2 x 400g tins plum tomatoes. I would recommend La Velle or Cirio
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 2-4 fresh bay leaves (optional); for fragrance
  • 1 tbsp miso paste; a good umami hit without being too over-powering
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper. For my Malaysians out there that needs a little spice
